What time is it in San Francisco?

What is the time in San Francisco if the time at Buffalo is 11:00 AM?

STEP 1: Calculate the time difference

Since San Francisco is in the time zone UTC-8 and Buffalo is in the tiem zone UTC-5, so the time difference between San Francisco and Buffalo is

8 - 5 =3 hours



STEP 2: Calculate the actual time

Since San Francisco is at the west side of Buffalo, so the time at San Francisco is earlier than the time at Buffalo

11:00 AM - 3 hours = 8:00 AM
